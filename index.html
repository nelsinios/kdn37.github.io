Hi there!

Welcome to the website of Thirty Seven Gang! We are a group of four students studying Introduction to Information Technology at RMIT University. This website acts as an interactive extension of the report submitted for the group assignment, which summarises who we are individually and what our career journey might look like, reporting on relevant industries and gaining insight from established professionals in the field.

Secondly, we as a team have also highlighted an understanding of evolving technology that has an even more relevant stance in everyday life, such as Blockchain, Cyber Security, Machine Learning and Robotics.

To complete this collective profile, we elected a project idea that we felt had real weight and more of a likelihood to be finessed for our final submission in assessment three. Information pertaining to that can be found under the "Project Idea".

For the sake of user friendliness, a sitemap can be found "HERE" and its coinciding rubric in an effort to make the visit to our website a seamless and easy to use experience. We hope you enjoy our presentation!
